Circle Times Table

Hi, I am Mohammad Tanjimur, I created Circle Times Table, a fun little project that turns multiplication into art.
Choose the number of points around the circle and select a multiplier. The site connects each point to its multiplication result. When the result goes past the final point, it wraps around the circle and continues.
Different numbers create surprising shapes, hearts, flowers, and beautiful patterns. Move the sliders, watch the animation, and discover your own mathematical artwork.
